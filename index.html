The Wonders of Houseplants

What a few houseplants can do for you

Hopefully I'll help you decide if this is something that could brighten your day

Some pros about the benefits of houseplants!

  • They are Beautiful - Houseplants are beautiful things you get to take care of them and in turn they offer you a host of benefits, the relationship people can have to caring for plants can really cause a positive impact on lives.
  • Help keep your space clean - Not only do they look great but they encourage you to keep a clean workspace, they cant thrive unless it's clear and well lit, this helps motivated you to make sure your space is too.
  • Positive for Mental Health - It's been proven that having plants is an effective way of keeping spirits high, healthy plants healthy mind.
  • Improve Air Clenliness - Some house plants are a great way to clean the air naturally in a room, these change based on plants however NASA has a great resource on plants that improve air quality!
  • They are mostly Cheap - Most houseplants come at a reasonable cost, you'll find that you can pick many up for less than the cost of a pint!
